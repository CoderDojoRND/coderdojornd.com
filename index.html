CoderDojo @ Ростов-на-Дону

We are unable to run CoderDojo sessions until we have a venue where we can hold regular sessions.

We are not looking for any donations to support us because CoderDojo is free for children to attend and mentors are volunteers. But we are looking for a free venue that we can use on Saturday evenings or Sunday afternoons/evenings.

We request parents who might be able to get us access to an office or class room available at the weekends to contact us by e-mailing us at coderdojornd@gmail.com.

To hold a CoderDojo session we need a wired or wireless network that the children can use to access the internet to download software and digital assets to their laptops. We plan to cater for 10-20 children in session and will be asking for the parents of children under the age of 13 to stay to help supervise those children.
